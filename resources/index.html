This page is designed to share resources that I have created or co-created. Most commonly, presentations given at various Mac admins conferences will have their slide decks and/or handouts posted or linked here. Articles posted on external sites as well as those self-published prior to the creation of my Mac Labs Blog (in 2017) are also included in the list.

Here are the resources in reverse chronological order:

Ongoing

  • The Mac Hardware/Software Obsolescence Chart that graphically represents which Macs support what operating systems and features. Available under a Creative Commons by-nc-sa Licence
  • My AutoPkg recipes repo in the AutoPkg project.
  • My GitHub repo, which is mostly populated with forks for pull requests and more recent conference presentation resources.
